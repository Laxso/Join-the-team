Guitarist Position, Now Open!!

Good day, future musician game dev!

Hey there! I’m Travis K. Nagel, the lead manager, scriptwriter, and programmer for our visual novel currently in development. We’re reopening a spot on our music team for someone who can play the guitar. We’re not looking for perfection. What we are looking for is someone with a basic understanding of guitar, in acoustic and electric. If you know the fundamentals and have a passion for improving, you’re already on the right track.

We’re also hoping to find someone who can write and create their own musical pieces. Nothing extreme or overly complex, just the ability to produce simple, original guitar work. You’ll be joining a supportive team of musicians who are helping shape the emotional core of our visual novel. This is a volunteer position, perfect for anyone looking to gain real-world experience in game development, contribute to something meaningful, and expand their musical portfolio.

We meet bi-weekly online, and we’re very flexible with deadlines. We understand school, work, and life getting busy, so we’re more than willing to work around your schedule.

If you’re interested, fill out the Google form, and feel free to reach out via Instagram or email. We’d love to hear from you and see what you can bring to the team.
