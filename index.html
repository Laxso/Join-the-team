Hey, You Wanna Become a Music Game Dev?

Hey there! I’m Travis K. Nagel, the lead manager, scriptwriter, and programmer for an exciting visual novel project currently in development. Our dedicated team is working hard to bring this game to life, featuring a character artist, game tester, and other talented creators. We’re aiming to launch the game on Steam by fall 2026!

Right now, we’re on the lookout for a musician to join our team someone who can play either guitar or piano and has a basic understanding of music theory. While it would be amazing if you’re skilled in both, having a solid grasp of just one is perfectly fine.

The style we’re aiming for leans toward classical, original compositions, and indie rock, with inspirations like Arctic Monkeys, 311, and Sublime, all while fitting the tone of a visual novel soundtrack. If you’ve got a portfolio, recordings, or past work to share, we’d love to hear it!

This is a mostly volunteer position perfect if you’re looking to gain real-world experience, expand your portfolio, and collaborate with a passionate, supportive team. While we’d love to offer full payment, we’re on a tight, out-of-pocket budget. That said, we’re open to discussing arrangements that work for both sides.

We meet bi-weekly online and keep deadlines flexible. We understand everyone has school, work, and life to balance, so we’ll work with your schedule.

If this sounds exciting, reach out on Instagram or by email we’d be thrilled to hear from you!

Hey, You Wanna Be A Video Game Background Dev?

Hey there! I’m Travis K. Nagel, the lead manager, scriptwriter, and programmer for an exciting visual novel project currently in development. Our dedicated team is working hard to bring this game to life, featuring people from a talented band, a game tester, and a character artist. We’re aiming to launch the game on Steam by fall 2026!

We’re on the lookout for a skilled digital background designer who’s familiar with visual novels. If you have a portfolio showcasing your work, we’d love to see it! To give you an idea of the process we’re looking for, it’s similar to what’s outlined in this tutorial: Anime Background Tutorial. Even if you don’t have much experience with visual novel backgrounds but have completed work in architecture or general background design, that’s perfectly fine. We’d much rather see polished work outside our niche than incomplete projects that closely match our vision.

This is mostly a volunteer position, perfect if you’re looking to gain real-world experience with a passionate and supportive team. While I’d love to offer payment, we’re currently working with a tight budget that is funded entirely out of pocket. That said, we can discuss payment possibilities, but please know it won’t be anything extravagant.

We meet bi-weekly online, and we’re super flexible with deadlines. We understand the importance of balancing work, school, and life, and we’re happy to accommodate your schedule.

If this sounds like something you’d love to be part of, hit me up on Instagram or email me. We’d be thrilled to hear from you!
